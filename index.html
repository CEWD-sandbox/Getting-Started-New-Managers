Support and resources designed for new managers

Congratulations on becoming a manager at Sydney Local Health District. You are important to buiding health teams and being leading performers, growing a workplace that everyone is proud of.

To support you in your new role, the District has developed the Getting Started for Managers Program and The Manager Orientation Checklist.

The Getting Started for Managers Program helps you to understand your core responsibilties as a manager. The program is based on the Management Accountability Framework and is designed to assist you in familiarising yourself with the District, your facilty and role.

The Manager Orientation Checklist helps to familiarise you with your new management role. It is designed to be completed within 3 months and contains:

  • Line manager briefing
  • Systems access information, applications and training
  • Policies and procedures
  • Virtual orientation program which includes:
    • Stakeholder briefings
    • CEWD education sessions for managers
